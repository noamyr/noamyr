Workshop
Algorithmic Queer Film Reading


Program developed by Noam Youngrak Son

Pop culture tends to represent a marginalized group, in this case, queer bodies, only in a particular way. Is it only tragedy that is destined for our endings? This workshop is to deconstruct those narratives and weave those again into a never-ending story with our queer hands. For your inspiration, I programmed a simple algorithm that analyzes how different concepts are associated with each other by counting how many times they were written in the same sentences. This tool can provide some meaningful hints about what the ‘average’ portrayal of queerness is in pop culture. When the same algorithm is used to predict the most probable phrase that will come after a specific phrase, it turns into a text generator that creates an endless stream of text by referencing the input. You can find this in the background of this page.
