The most important source of inspiration for its layout was the book “Diagrammatic Writing” written by Johanna Drucker and published by Onomatopee. The website fills the whole screen with a block of text and makes the rest of the contents float on the grid that the monospaced paragraph naturally creates. The background text can be scrolled infinitely due to the generative algorithm that creates new text from every commissioned writing I’ve done.

In addition to the main website, there’s a special page for my book-making practices called “Cathair Press.” It presents publications produced in my domestic space shared with two cats. The books inevitably contain feline hairs, therefore not suitable for readers with cat allergies. Cathair Press page uses the genome sequence of a domestic cat provided by U.S. National Center for Biotechnology Information as its background text. It can also be scrolled almost endlessly because of its enormous length, 2521860000 base pairs.
